BIO

Yuqing LIU’s research interests encompass cultural studies and film studies, with a particular emphasis on the intersection between the digital and the humanistic. Currently, she is focusing on affectivity in virtual reality (VR) films, exploring how these films evoke affective experiences in viewers through immersive digital environments. She earned her BA in Chinese Language and Literature and a BFA in Art Theory from Peking University. Liu further advanced her studies at the same institution, completing an MA in Theory of Literature and Art. Additionally, she has a passion for poetry writing.
